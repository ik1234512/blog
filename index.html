The Apex Periodical

Inspiring The Next Generation Of Readers

The Apex Periodical is a youth run online magazine focused on inviting the adolescents of today into a world where your senses are assaulted by information from every direction.

Nowadays, we’re very particular about the information that we consume; namely because it comes from textbooks or is related to the career that we’re pursuing. We want to take our readers back to the times when we would spend lazy afternoons reading General Knowledge Encyclopediae and just be amazed at how diverse and vast and unfathomably complex the world is. We hope you can lose yourself in our articles, even if just for a little while.
